SERP Features

In the SEOGUN service, you can track the appearance of your website as per selected keywords in the extended issuance blocks. We have chosen 14 main blocks for tracking.

Adwords (Top) is a block of contextual advertising at the top of the search results page.

Adwords (Bottom) is a block of contextual advertising at the end of the search results page.

Shopping Results is a block of advertising results for commercial queries.

Featured Snippet is an information block with answers to key queries located at the beginning of the search results.

Image Pack is a block of results displayed as a horizontal row of links to images, for which an image search is carried out.

Knowledge Panel is a block of detailed and structured information about objects. It is displayed to the right of organic search results. It displays information

Local Pack is a block of regional issuance. It shows results for a narrow query with location.popular brands, names, objects, people.carried out.

Related Questions (people also ask) is a block displayed on search pages, usually this is a part of information websites that have a similar query.

Tweet -is a block displaying tweets of popular people, jumping to highly specialized questions with a long tail or issued, when searching for people.

Video is a block of results that are displayed with the title of the video, its duration, description, upload date and username.

Top stories is a news block. It is displayed for queries that have news in Google Search, now in Google news.

Reviews is a block rating that complements the regular snippet with stars below the page URL.

Site Links is a block with additional links to the website, which are located immediately below the main link, and up to 8 bright links.

Related Searches Related Searches are multiple search queries related to the searched keyword. Classically, they are 8 related queries located at the end of the search page. These results can help users to better satisfy their search interest, and for a SEO specialist or webmaster, this is a way to find the most optimal keywords for the semantic kernel. Recently, this block has been expanding and consists not only of key queries, but also combines links to persons, information about companies, or is supplemented with images and descriptions.

The blocks that your website falls into are marked in red.
